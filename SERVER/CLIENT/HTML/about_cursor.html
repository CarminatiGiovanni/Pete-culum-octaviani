About CURSOR

How was the project born?

9/17/2021 - Bergamo Italy

We made cursor from 17 to 19 September 2021 for the "hackaton 2021" event. The track spoke of the difficulties that can be encountered in traveling in 2021. On the evening of September 17, we thought a lot about the problems we face every day. We selected a couple of interesting ideas and in the end we decided to create a website that would help the users who take the bus every morning. We divided the tasks and from the morning of the 18th to the evening of the 19th we worked continuously on the project.


How does it work?

The site is completely free and does not require user login. from the home, simply press the "find your bus" button and then choose the desired bus. From there a screen opens from which you can see details in real time such as how busy it is or if it is late on your bus or send feedback.
